Battery, Weather, and Time on the HomeScreen<br>
Decorate your Home Screen with this XenHTML widget! This widget features sections for
the current time, apps, your daily schedule, and information regarding battery/weather, with modules displayed
in a unique split-diagonal design.<br>
Place your apps in the designated spot using free tools such as Atria and BlankIcons!
