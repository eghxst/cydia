Split your HomeScreen in two!<br>
Decorate your Home Screen with this XenHTML widget! This widget will split your homescreen
in half, with your apps on one half and information such as events, reminders, date, time, and weather on the other!<br>
Place your apps in the designated spot using free tools such as Atria and BlankIcons!
