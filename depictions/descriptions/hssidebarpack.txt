Battery and Date on a sidebar on the HomeScreen<br>
Decorate your Home Screen with this XenHTML widget! This widget gives you a designated place
to put your apps, along with a sidebar that displays Battery and Date information.<br>
Place your apps in the designated spot using free tools such as Atria and BlankIcons!
