Date, Time, Battery and Weather HomeScreen<br>
Decorate your Home Screen with this XenHTML widget! This widget features four pill-shaped modules
that display the date, time, battery, and weather. It also features a designated area to place your apps.<br>
Place your apps in the designated spot using free tools such as Atria and BlankIcons!
